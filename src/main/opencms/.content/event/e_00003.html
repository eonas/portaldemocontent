<![CDATA[Flower Exhibition Oldenburg]]> Every year in the beginning of march the Flower Exhibition takes place in the Weser-Ems-Hall. An average 40.000 people visit the Flower Exhibition. The main attraction is the 5.000 m² large exhibition hall. On another 11.000 m² you find around 170 exhibitors at the Gardening- and Consumer Trade Fair.
