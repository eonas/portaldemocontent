<![CDATA[International Horticultural Trade Fair]]> International Horticultural Trade Fair in Tulln on the Danube is Austria's largest Flower show and one of the largest european exhibitions on gardening und floristics. It originated from a traditional flowerparade in 1954 and first took place in 1967.
