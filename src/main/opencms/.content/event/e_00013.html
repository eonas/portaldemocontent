<![CDATA["Symphony in Green" in Ansfelden]]> This year's theme of the Upper Austrian Garden Festival in Ansfelden is "Symphony in Green" and refers to Ansfelden's famous son, the composer Anton Bruckner. The Garden Festival features 4 main themes according to a symphony called allegro, scherzo, adagio and finale. The school of green professions in Ritzlhof invites visitors to the floral carpets and gardening ideas of its students during the warm half of the year from april till october.
