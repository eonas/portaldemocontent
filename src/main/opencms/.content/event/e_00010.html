<![CDATA[Bundesgartenschau 2011 in Koblenz]]> The Bundesgartenschau (abbr. BUGA) is the biannual Federal horticulture show in Germany. It also covers topics like landscaping. Taking place in different cities, the location changes in a two-year cycle.

The 2011 BUGA is arranged by Koblenz from April 15 to October 16. One of the attractions of this show will be the Koblenz Cable Car, Germanys biggest aerial lift.

With a high budget, extensive landscape architectures and gardenings are realized that support the regional development objectives in the respective cites.

Main sites are Ehrenbreitstein fortress with a great variety of exhibitions and the Blumenhof courtyard where the river Mosel ends into the Rhine. Together with the Electoral Palace there's 48 hectars covered by the exhibition.
